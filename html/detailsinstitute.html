About Kidzee Duliajan

Kidzee Duliajan, established in 2025, is a nurturing and child-friendly preschool and kindergarten dedicated to providing a strong foundation for early childhood learning. Located at Christian Basti, Near TD+ Colony, Duliajan – 786602, the school is committed to shaping young minds through a safe, joyful, and activity-based learning environment.


At Kidzee Duliajan, the focus is on holistic development, ensuring that children grow not only academically but also emotionally, socially, and creatively. The preschool follows a structured yet flexible early-learning approach designed to help children develop essential skills such as communication, motor coordination, problem-solving, confidence, and curiosity. Through a blend of interactive activities, play-based learning, storytelling, music, art, and hands-on exploration, children are encouraged to discover their abilities at their own pace.
