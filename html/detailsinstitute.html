About Kidzee Duliajan

Kidzee Duliajan, established in 2025, is a nurturing and child-friendly preschool and kindergarten dedicated to providing a strong foundation for early childhood learning. Located at Christian Basti, Near TD+ Colony, Duliajan – 786602, the school is committed to shaping young minds through a safe, joyful, and activity-based learning environment.

At Kidzee Duliajan, the focus is on holistic development, ensuring that children grow not only academically but also emotionally, socially, and creatively. The preschool follows a structured yet flexible early-learning approach designed to help children develop essential skills such as communication, motor coordination, problem-solving, confidence, and curiosity. Through a blend of interactive activities, play-based learning, storytelling, music, art, and hands-on exploration, children are encouraged to discover their abilities at their own pace.

The school is guided by dedicated educators and caretakers under the leadership of Pranjal Deka and Violina Kalita, who ensure that each child receives personalized attention and care. Their warm and supportive teaching approach helps children feel comfortable, secure, and motivated to learn every day.

Kidzee Duliajan places high importance on creating a clean, hygienic, and child-safe environment. Classrooms are designed to be bright, engaging, and equipped with age-appropriate learning materials that stimulate imagination and creativity. The preschool also focuses on helping children develop discipline, good habits, and social values that will benefit them throughout their schooling years.

With a mission to make early education meaningful, enjoyable, and accessible, Kidzee Duliajan aims to prepare young learners for a smooth transition into formal schooling while giving them the confidence to explore the world with curiosity and enthusiasm.
